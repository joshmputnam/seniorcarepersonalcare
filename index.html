Providing Compassionate In-Home Care for Hospice and End-of-Life Patients

At Senior Care Personal Care, we understand the challenges and emotional toll that caring for a loved one facing end-of-life circumstances can bring. We're here to provide the support and assistance you need during this difficult time. Our dedicated caregivers offer in-home care for hospice patients and those near the end of life, focusing on providing comfort, compassion, and assistance with daily living activities.

Our Services

We offer a range of services to improve the quality of life for your loved ones.

Companionship and Daily Living Assistance

Our caregivers are not healthcare professionals, but they are compassionate and experienced in assisting with basic activities of daily living, ensuring your loved one's comfort and well-being. We offer support with:

Personal Hygiene

Showers

Toileting

Dressing

Basic Meal Preparation

Feeding

We can assist with ambulation & transfers

Length of Care Service

We understand that each individual's needs are unique. Our services can be tailored to meet your specific requirements. Whether you need a few hours of care or around-the-clock assistance, we are here to provide the level of care that suits your situation. Our minimum visit duration is 3 hours, and we can provide care for up to 12 hours per shift.

Hourly Care: Flexible, on-demand care that suits your schedule and needs

24-Hour Care with Multiple Shifts: Round-the-clock care provided by our dedicated caregivers in shifts

Live-In Care: A caregiver resides in your loved one's home, offering continuous support

Weekends & Holidays: Our services are available seven days a week, including holidays
